Taiyaki

Taiyaki in it's natural habitat!

Taiyaki is a popular Japanese pastry, most recognizable for being fish shaped!

This wonder is achieved by heating a mold filled with flour-based batter and later adding the filling of your choice!

If you dare to dream, it tastes even better with ice cream!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup All-purpose Flour
  • 2 tbsp Melted Butter
  • 2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 3 tbsp Sugar
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1 tbsp Vegetable/Canola Oil
  • Filling of Choice

Steps

  1. Sift flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt into a large bowl.
  2. In a seperate bowl, whisk the egg, milk and melted butter.
  3. Combine both bowls and whisk well.
  4. Pour the batter into something pourable like a measuring cup.
  5. Heat up the Taiyaki mold pan and grease it up using vegetable/canola oil and a brush.
  6. Fill the pan approx. 60% full over medium-low heat.
  7. Put filling on top and center of the batter that is currently in the pan mold.
  8. Cover the filling with more batter.
  9. Close the lid and immediately turn/flip. Cook for 2-2.5 minutes on each side. Flip and cook a final 2-2.5 minutes.
  10. Open and check to see if Taiyaki is a golden color. Let it cool afterwards.
