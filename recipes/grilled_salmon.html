Grilled Salmon

Credit: recipesfrommymom.com

Grilled Salmon in it's natural habitat!

Grilled Salmon is a simple yet flavourful dish!

A must for all seafood enthusiasts.

As alluded to in its name, access to a grill is required.

**This ingredients list and preperation was found at allrecipes! Check them out for more amazing recipes!**

Ingredients

  • 11/2 lbs Salmon Fillets
  • To Taste Lemon Pepper
  • To Taste Garlic Powder
  • To Taste Salt
  • 1/3 cup Soy Sauce
  • 1/3 cup Brown Sugar
  • 1/3 cup Water
  • 1/4 cup Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Season salmon fillets with lemon pepper, garlic powder, and salt.
  2. Stir soy sauce, brown sugar, water, and vegetable oil together in a small bowl until sugar is dissolved.
  3. Place fish in a large resealable plastic bag; add soy sauce mixture, seal, and turn to coat.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
  5. Preheat grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
  6. Place salmon on the preheated grill, and discard marinade. Cook salmon until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 6 to 8 minutes per side.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
